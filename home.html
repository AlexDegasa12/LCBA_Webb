Welcome to Laguna College of Business and Arts

LCBA, for 9 decades, has provided quality education to the youth and adults of Calamba City and beyond. Through the years, it has embraced the changes in the educational system and innovated accordingly. Inspired by the principles of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, it has transformed many lives and still continues up to the present.

Choose LCBA

LCBA, a Private Higher Education Institution (PHEI), is recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd) and Commision on Higher Education (CHED), certified by the Private Education Assistance Committee (PEAC), and accredited by the Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA).
